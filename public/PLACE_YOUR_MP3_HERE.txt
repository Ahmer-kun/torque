=== HOW TO UPLOAD YOUR BACKGROUND MP3 MUSIC ===

1. Name your MP3 file precisely "music.mp3" (all lowercase).
2. Upload/drag-and-drop that "music.mp3" file directly into this "/public" directory.
3. The romantic confession app will automatically find it, play it instead of the procedural synthesizer, and show a beautiful "Custom Soundtrack" pill representation!

If no "music.mp3" is found, the system automatically falls back to an elegant live atmospheric synthesis (chord generator) so the music never crashes or fails! Enjoy!
